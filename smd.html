BULB FLATS

SMD Steel partners with world class mills to supply bulb flats in a full wide range of sizes and grades. Large inventory at our Mississippi plant available for immediate delivery to support our customers.

  • Full size range: b x t (mm) –80 x 5 to 430 x 20
  • Lengths: 40 ft (stock); 20ft-52ft (mill direct orders)
  • Grades: ABS A, B, D, A32, A36, AH / DH 36, EH36
  • Standards: EN 10067 –1997

Our bulb flats are approved by various shipbuilding classifications including ABS, Lloyds, GL, DNV, Lloyds etc.

processing

sustainability

At SMD Steel, we firmly believe in playing our part for a sustainable future. Our modern and highly energy efficient plate processing equipment has an automated fume extracting system installed which is designed to safely capture the smoke generated by the plasma cutting heads during plate burning. Our 35-acre footprint of the facility has more than 1/2 of the site preserved with natural trees to combat greenhouse gases. Recycling bins are located strategically throughout our facility, encouraging teammates to recycle as much content as possible thereby reducing the number of landfills. Every little change contributes manyfold towards carbon neutrality, leaving a healthy planet for our future generations.
