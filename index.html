About Us

About Us

Pinnakl is an engineering and technology services company based in Memphis, Tennessee. We specialize in metallurgical services, failure analysis, and solving complex manufacturing engineering challenges. With our experience and expertise, we make solutions simple and effective.
We serve industries including heavy manufacturing, oil and gas, energy, transportation, and medical devices, delivering innovative solutions that drive efficiency and reliability.

Our team is committed to delivering tailored services designed to meet the specific needs of our manufacturing clients. Over the years, we have provided a range of services to support the success and growth of businesses in various sectors.

We use modern solutions to minimize project costs while ensuring compliance with all quality and safety standards.

Services

1. Product development

We have more than a decade of experience in developing and testing various products. Whether it's developing a metal product, designing a material grade, or helping our clients with process qualifications, we will not only stand by you throughout the process but also ensure we meet the deadlines.

2. Business development

We have done multiple market analyses, competition analyses, business case reviews, and helped our customers achieve their goals.

3. Metallurgical and mechanical failure analysis

We have conducted numerous failure investigations in the oil and gas, automotive, and heavy industries. These investigations involve advanced microscopic analysis using SEM/EDS, mechanical testing, and in-depth evaluations. Our experience includes analyzing failed oil and gas pipelines, automotive components, heavy equipment parts, and various in-process failures.

4. Writing and reviewing specifications

We have worked across multiple manufacturing sectors, including oil and gas (line pipes), automotive, HVAC, and power transmission. Our expertise spans from developing specifications to reviewing them in accordance with updated regulations. Additionally, we can streamline the specification review process by integrating necessary automation.

5. Solving complex manufacturing problems

Achieving high efficiencies, reducing quality costs, and optimizing the supply chain are some of the key manufacturing challenges we have tackled. Our approach includes methods such as value stream mapping, testing, and developing measurement tools. We prioritize in-house solutions, working closely with our customers to ensure they take ownership and implement them effectively.

6. Optimizing supply chain

Evaluating suppliers, performing regular audits, and setting processes to evaluate the suppliers are some of the projects we have done in the past.
