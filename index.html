About Us

About Us?

Pinnakl is an engineering services company based in Memphis, Tennessee, specializing in metallurgical services, along with engineering and consulting expertise for clients in industries such as heavy manufacturing, oil and gas, energy, transportation, and medical devices.

Our team is committed to delivering tailored services designed to meet the specific needs of our manufacturing clients. Over the years, we have provided a range of services to support the success and growth of businesses in the various sector's.

We use modern solutions to minimize project costs while maintaining all safety standards.

Services

1. Product development

We have more than a decade of experience developing and testing various products. Let it be developing a steel product, designing a material grade, or helping our client making the product development seamless. We will not only stand by you throughout the process but make sure we meet the deadlines.

2. Business development

We have done multiple market analyses, competition analyses, business case reviews, and helped our customers achieve their goals.

3. Metallurgical and mechanical failure analysis

We have performed multiple failure analyses in oil and gas, automotive, and heavy industries. These failure analyses involve microscopic work including SEM/EDS, mechanical tests, and detailed analysis. We have looked into oil and gas failed pipes, automotive parts, heavy equipment parts, and also some in-process failures.

4. Writing and reviewing specifications

We have worked in multiple manufacturing sectors including oil and gas, automotive, HVAC, power transmission, etc. We have handled projects from developing a specification to reviewing the specifications as per changed regulations. We can also look into making the spec review process more seamless by adding needed automation.

5. Solving long-term manufacturing problems

Achieving high efficiencies, reducing quality costs, and optimizing the supply chain are some of the manufacturing projects we have solved in the past. These include using multiple approaches like value stream mapping, testing, and creating measurement tools. We believe in developing in-house solutions with the involvement of our customers so that they own the solutions and implement them effectively.

6. Optimizing supply chain

Evaluating suppliers, performing regular audits, and setting processes to evaluate the suppliers are some of the projects we have done in the past.
